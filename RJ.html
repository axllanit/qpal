Gun Park (박종건 Park Jong Geon) is a major antagonist in Lookism. He is a fighter of legendary status and the personal bodyguard of the HNH Chairman's daughter, Crystal Choi. He is also one of the "10 Geniuses" raised by Charles Choi, as well as being the heir to the Yamazaki Syndicate in Japan, earning the title of White Demon.
